Frequently Asked Questions

Is the FunS app free? +

Yes, the FunS app is completely free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases or hidden fees. You can enjoy games and earn tokens at no cost.

What devices are supported? +

FunS supports Android 8.0 and above. You can install the APK directly or access via web browser on both PC and mobile devices.

How do I use tokens earned from games? +

FUNS tokens earned from games can be swapped for other cryptocurrencies via FunSwap DEX in the app, stored in your wallet, or used whenever needed.
