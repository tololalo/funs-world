Features

Why FUNS Talk?

No email, no phone number required. Your conversations stay private, and you can send crypto right from the chat.

🔐

Full E2E Encryption

Only the sender and receiver can read messages. Military-grade encryption that even our servers can't decrypt.

👛

Sign Up with a Wallet

A wallet is auto-created when you install the app. Log in with PIN or biometrics. Connect existing wallets like MetaMask too.

💸

Send Crypto in Chat

Send BNB or FunS tokens right in a conversation. Just type /send and you're done. No copy-pasting addresses.

🎫

Token-Gated Channels

Create premium channels accessible only to NFT or FunS token holders. Build your own exclusive community.

⛽

Zero Gas Fee Worries

Basic messaging is completely free. Blockchain fees? FUNS Talk covers them for you. Just chat like any other app.

👥

Group Chat & File Sharing

Share files and media securely in encrypted group chats. Highlight messages with Super Chat to stand out.

How to Use

Getting Started is Easy

As simple as any messenger. Blockchain runs quietly in the background.

1

Install the App

Download and your wallet is created automatically. No extra setup needed.

2

PIN / Biometrics

Log in with fingerprint or a simple PIN. No seed phrases to memorize.

3

Add Friends & Chat

Add friends by wallet address or nickname. All conversations are encrypted automatically.

4

Send & Join

Send crypto right in chat and join token-gated channels for exclusive communities.

💬 Run Commands in Chat

/send Send FunS to a friend
/tip Tip a message
/burn Voluntary burn (community contribution)
/gate Token-gate a channel
/balance Check your FunS balance
