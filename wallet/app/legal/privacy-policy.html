Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Introduction

FunS Wallet ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Data Collection

We believe in minimal data collection. FunS Wallet operates with a privacy-first approach:

  • Local Storage Only: All wallet data, including private keys, seed phrases, and transaction history, is stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Account Registration: You do not need to create an account or provide personal information to use FunS Wallet.
  • No Server-Side Storage: We do not transmit, store, or have access to your wallet data on any server.

3. Information We Collect

FunS Wallet may collect the following limited information:

  • Blockchain Transaction Data: Public transaction hashes and addresses are recorded on the blockchain (this is not controlled by us).
  • Anonymous Analytics: We may collect anonymized, non-identifiable usage analytics to improve app performance and user experience. This does not include personal or wallet information.
  • Device Identifiers: Temporary device identifiers for API rate limiting purposes only.

4. Third-Party Services

FunS Wallet integrates with external services for enhanced functionality:

  • CoinGecko API: For real-time cryptocurrency price data. Visit their privacy policy at https://www.coingecko.com/en/privacy
  • Blockchain RPC Nodes: To interact with blockchain networks (BSC, Ethereum, etc.). These are decentralized services that may log transaction requests.
  • QR Code Libraries: Open-source libraries for scanning QR codes (processed entirely on-device).

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. Please review their privacy policies.

5. Camera Usage

FunS Wallet requests camera access exclusively for:

  • Scanning QR codes for wallet addresses and payment requests
  • All QR code processing occurs on your device; camera data is never transmitted or stored

6. Biometric Data

If you enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint):

  • Local Processing Only: Biometric data is processed entirely on your device using the operating system's secure enclave or trusted execution environment.
  • Never Transmitted: We never access, store, or transmit biometric data to any server.
  • Device Control: Your device's operating system manages all biometric authentication, not FunS Wallet.

7. User Rights and Data Control

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • Data Deletion: All data stored by FunS Wallet is deleted when you uninstall the application.
  • Self-Custody: You are the sole custodian of your private keys and wallet data.
  • No Account Dependence: FunS Wallet does not maintain accounts or records that persist after app uninstallation.

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data on-device
  • Secure local storage using platform-provided key management systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No transmission of private keys or seed phrases

9. Children's Privacy

FunS Wallet is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will promptly delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the revised policy in the App with an updated "Effective Date." Your continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: funsxofficial@gmail.com

Organization: FunS Wallet Team

12. Compliance

FunS Wallet complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including GDPR (for EU residents) and other regional privacy requirements.

This Privacy Policy is provided "as-is" for informational purposes. For the most current version, please refer to the App or our official website.

당사는 최소한의 데이터 수집 원칙을 따르고 있습니다. FunS Wallet은 개인정보 보호 우선 방식으로 운영됩니다:

  • 로컬 저장소만 사용: 개인 키, 시드 문구, 거래 내역을 포함한 모든 지갑 데이터는 사용자 기기에만 저장됩니다.
  • 회원가입 불필요: FunS Wallet을 사용하기 위해 계정을 생성하거나 개인정보를 제공할 필요가 없습니다.
  • 서버 저장 없음: 당사는 지갑 데이터를 전송, 저장하지 않으며 서버에서 접근할 수 없습니다.

3. 수집하는 정보

FunS Wallet은 다음의 제한된 정보만 수집할 수 있습니다:

  • 블록체인 거래 데이터: 공개 거래 해시 및 주소는 블록체인에 기록됩니다 (이는 당사가 통제하지 않음).
  • 익명 분석: 앱 성능 및 사용자 경험 개선을 위해 익명화되고 식별 불가능한 사용 통계를 수집할 수 있습니다. 이는 개인정보나 지갑 정보를 포함하지 않습니다.
  • 기기 식별자: API 속도 제한 목적의 임시 기기 식별자만 사용됩니다.

4. 제3자 서비스

FunS Wallet은 향상된 기능을 위해 외부 서비스와 통합됩니다:

  • CoinGecko API: 실시간 암호화폐 가격 데이터 제공. https://www.coingecko.com/en/privacy의 개인정보 보호정책을 참조하세요.
  • 블록체인 RPC 노드: 블록체인 네트워크 (BSC, Ethereum 등)와의 상호작용. 분산화된 서비스로 거래 요청을 로깅할 수 있습니다.
  • QR 코드 라이브러리: QR 코드 스캔을 위한 오픈소스 라이브러리 (기기 내에서만 처리).

FunS Wallet은 카메라 접근을 다음의 목적으로만 요청합니다:
